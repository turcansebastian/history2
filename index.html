Chronology of Napoleon and the Napoleonic Wars
Year Event Details
1769 Napoleon's Birth Born in Corsica to a modest family.
1785 Graduation from École Militaire Commissioned as a second lieutenant in the artillery.
1789 French Revolution Begins Marked the end of the Ancien Régime.
1793 Siege of Toulon Napoleon distinguishes himself and is promoted to brigadier general.
1795 Suppression of Royalist Uprising Napoleon gains fame for quelling the revolt (13 Vendémiaire).
1796–1797 Italian Campaign Napoleon defeats Austrian forces, forcing the Treaty of Campo Formio.
1798–1799 Egyptian Campaign Victories in Egypt overshadowed by a naval defeat at the Battle of the Nile.
1805 Battle of Austerlitz Napoleon defeats Austria and Russia, ending the Third Coalition.
1812 Russian Campaign Napoleon’s invasion of Russia ends in disastrous retreat.
1815 Battle of Waterloo Napoleon’s final defeat; exiled to Saint Helena.
1821 Death of Napoleon Dies in exile on Saint Helena.
